About Mary Belanger

Mary Belanger is a Professional photographer who lives in Ontario Canada, she has been doing photography for about 8 years and has vast experience which includes collaboration works with other photographers and also travelling to other states and countries to work. She combines her photography degree with her marketing degree to find the right balance between creativity and proper advertisement. Mary is an experienced all round photographer and moved to Ontario at the end of 2020.

You can checkout her recent works and experiences below. She considers photography as her passion more than profession.
